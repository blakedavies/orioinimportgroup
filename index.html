About

Orion Import Group works directly with businesses to both source and import high quality products. We manage all the work, so you can manage your business.

On the ground in China, Orion Import Group works with three factories, and central office with over one hundred and twenty employees. We can import everything from garden pots to mining machinery.

Orion Import Group offers a wide variety of products available for import. We put the best product design, manufacturing and production from overseas factories into the palm of your hand.

Services

Importing Services

OIG collaborates with your business for all of your importing needs. Logistics are taken care by both the team here in Australian, and our team over in China. Once your order has been placed with us, you can trust that all of the steps necessary for importing are taken care of.

Manufacturing

OIG’s China office has been operating for over a decade. In that time staff have become experts in their respective fields. Through forging strong relationships we are able to offer a unique opportunity in negotiations direct with the factories. In addition to our unlimited sourcing capability, we also own and operate a metal fabrication facility.

Quality Control

OIG assign staff trained in quality control to all orders. This ensures all products delivered are of the highest quality. Any issues identified will be handled as a matter of priority. Orion Import Group guarantees finding the best method to compensate for any losses.

Product Development

Because of our immense product sourcing and manufacturing experience, OIG are not limited to merely finding existing products. We can assist you in new product development. Innovation is where success lies because your product is unique and only you will sell it.
